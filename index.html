Our Story

St. Thomas to Seattle

The base lemonade was at every family event — from reunions to baby showers, right next to the Sorrel and Tamarind drink. When my mother passed, I craved this lemonade and realized no one in Seattle offered Caribbean-style lemonade. So I brought it here.

Made in honor of La La 🍋
