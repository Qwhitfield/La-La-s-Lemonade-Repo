Lemonade

Classic

Fresh lemon, real sugar, water. The original Caribbean-style — clean, tart, and refreshing. The base everything starts from.

Flavored

Creamy Specials

Classic base plus cream — silky, rich, and totally indulgent. Our condensed milk lemonades fall in this category.

Fruity Specials

Flavored base with fresh fruit added in — muddled and shaken for a bold, layered taste. Extra tropical.

Customer Favorites

Mixing Suggestions

Please note: We don't sell alcohol. These are popular mixing recipes our customers enjoy with our lemonade at home. Always drink responsibly and in accordance with local laws.

Shandy

Light & refreshing

  • Your favorite beer (lager or wheat beer works great)
  • La La's Classic Lemonade
  • Mix 1:1 over ice

Spiked Lemonade

The classic

  • Rum (white or coconut rum)
  • La La's Classic or Flavored Lemonade
  • Garnish with a lemon slice

Lazy Lemon Drop

Citrus forward

  • Vodka
  • Cointreau or triple sec
  • La La's Classic Lemonade
  • Shake over ice & strain

Mocktail 🌿 Non-Alcoholic

Zero proof, all flavor

  • A few dashes of bitters
  • Fresh rosemary sprig (muddled)
  • Tart cherry juice
  • La La's Classic Lemonade
  • Serve over ice with a rosemary garnish
